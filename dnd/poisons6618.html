Rhododendron

[ Rhododendron ]
ingested (vegetal) - 400 gp
The poison made from this plant causes severe diarrhea and vomiting.
A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until the end of its next long rest. Its Strength score is also temporarily reduced by 2 while poisoned in this way.
