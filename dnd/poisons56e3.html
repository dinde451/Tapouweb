Fluna

[ Fluna ]
injury (venom) - 200 gp
Fluna is a swamp beetle that becomes aggressive only during breeding periods. Administered at high doses, its venom is very dangerous.
A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 10 Constitution saving throw or take 7 (2d6) poison damage.
