Giant Toad poison

[ Poison de crapaud géant ]
injury (venom) - 200 gp
This poison must be harvested from a dead or incapacitated giant toad.
A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 11 Constitution saving throw or take 5 (1d10) poison damage.
