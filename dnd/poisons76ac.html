Giant scorpion venom

[ Venin de scorpion géant ]
injury (venom) - 1000 gp
This poison must be harvested from a dead or incapacitated giant scorpion.
A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 12 Constitution saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
