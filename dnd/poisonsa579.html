Lomat's Illusion

[ Illusion de Lomat ]
ingested (mixture) - 500 gp
Tasteless liquid or sand-like powder, once ingested this poison causes the victim to believe that it is being watched continuously.
A creature subjected to this poison must make a DD 15 Wisdom saving throw. In case of failure, the creature becomes completely paranoid (frightened) for 6d10 minutes.
