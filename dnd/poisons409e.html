Lich dust

[ Cendres de liche ]
ingested (mixture) - 400 gp
This black syrup with a slight taste of ashes can neutralize its victim in a few seconds. It is usually added to the food of the victim and a few drops are enough, which explains its low price for a mixture.
A creature subjected to this poison must make a DC 14 Constitution saving throw, taking 10 (4d4) poison damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
