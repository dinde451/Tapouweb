Silvered wine

[ Vin argenté ]
ingested (toxin) - 400 gp
This poison is a silvered liquor with a very fine taste. In a completely unexplained way, it does not affect halflings and gnomes.
A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or fall into fits of laughter during 2d4 rounds.
