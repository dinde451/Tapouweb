Thrum

[ Thrum ]
ingested (toxin) - 700 gp
This light blue liquid smells like lemon. It puts the victim in an advanced state of alcoholism.
A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 14 Constitution saving throw or be poisoned until its finishes a long rest. While poisoned, the creature's Dexterity score is reduced by 1d4.
