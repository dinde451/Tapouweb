Cybella pollen

[ Pollen de cybella ]
inhaled (vegetal) - 300 gp
Breathing the pollen of this flower bring on hallucinations (appearance of enemy creatures, feeling persecuted and/or being watched) to the victim.
A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 12 Constitution saving throw or be frightened for 2d4 rounds.
