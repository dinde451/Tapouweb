Ungol dust

[ Cendres d'ungol ]
inhaled (mixture) - 600 gp
This poison looks like dusty red-brown flakes. The smell they given off when consumed causes serious respiratory lesions which are never fully recovered.
A creature subjected to this poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or its Constitution score is reduced by 2. The reduction lasts until removed by the greater restoration spell or other magic.
